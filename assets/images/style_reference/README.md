## Visual rules

1. **First-person halls** — long one-point perspective corridors/rooms; player hand or weapon in the foreground.
2. **Lo-fi CRT** — 320×200 internal res, nearest upscale, scanlines, vignette, dither/grain.
3. **Darkness-forward** — most of the frame in deep shadow; light pools mark path and threat.
4. **Uncanny accents** — neon pink/magenta/cyan/purple on the hand and supernatural FX against blood-red, toxic green, and teal environments.
5. **Limited contrast palette** — per-floor cluster: Red → Green → Teal → Black (void).

## Do not

- Bright, clean labs or flat modern UI lighting.
- Smooth HD gradients without dither/pixel crunch.
- Card-like or menu-chrome clutter in the play view.
